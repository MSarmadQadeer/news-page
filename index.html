International Sports Festival

  Welcome to the International Sports Festival page! Here we have news, links, and other information related to the International Sports Festival which is going to be held in America this year.

Football

  Football Championship will be in Boulder, CO on May 16-18, 2016. The event will be one week earlier than usual, and will be held in conjunction with CNN Sports. The (Ultimate Players Association) UPA is partnering with CNN and Grass Roots Ultimate to host the event.

  Primary School will be moving east, at least relatively speaking, to Independence, MO. The tournament will be held on May 10-11, 2016 and will be hosted by the City of Wales and Recreation Department. and Kansus City.

Rugby

  Rugby matches will also be held in collaboration with UPA at Boulderwood place on Sunday, January 20, 2016 at 5:30 pm. Please contact UPA executive director Mike Hew at mh@upa.org if you plan to attend so that we can plan appropriately.

Polo

  Polo will be held in the right park of the city common area. Special Kit will be provided for free to last year winners but rest of the teams have to place their Order. Order can be placed at the main sports complex by Friday this week. Fitness DVD will be provided for FREE!

Baseball

  Baseball is part of the international Sports week since 2017. Since then events related to these are held in coordination with the city council in the main sports complex located at the city center. This year sixteen teams from different Universities all over the world are participating in this competition. Organiors pay to the city council for all the arrangements they make on our behalf.

  For those interested in the visiting the country side, they can submit their request at the Gildhall. The subsequent decision(s) will be made by the UPA administration and Board of Directors.
